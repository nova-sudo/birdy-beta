# Playbook: Budget reallocation

**Reach for it when:** the account has a wide CPL spread (winners and losers coexist). Shifting budget
from losers to proven winners is usually the highest-leverage, lowest-risk move. Quantify it so the
recommendation is concrete.

## Method

1. **Identify candidates.** Ad sets consistently **below** target CPL with quality + headroom (scale
   candidates), and those consistently **above** with no fixable cause (cut candidates). Weight by
   spend — this only matters where real dollars sit.
2. **Estimate the reclaim.** Budget currently on cut candidates that's producing above-target or
   zombie leads.
3. **Redeploy in ~20–30% steps** to scale candidates (`scaling.md`). Project **conservatively** —
   assume some CPL creep as you scale (winners rarely hold their exact CPL at 2× budget).
4. **State assumptions.** Held CPL, no fatigue, same close rate. Reallocation projections are
   directional.

## Framing

Say it as: *"shift ~$X/day from A,B into C,D; expect roughly N more leads at roughly $Y CPL; re-check
in 3–4 days."* Never a false-precision guarantee — give the direction and the recheck point, not a
promise.
